**ğŸ”‘ Key Findings (The Contradictions)**

The case was solved by proving three critical contradictions in the killer's digital data:

False Alibi: The suspect's statement was directly contradicted by the time-stamped keycard access logs placing them at the crime scene.

Scene Communication: A phone call was made during the exact 10-minute window the suspect was logged inside the CEO's office.

Physical Corroboration: The keycard log anomalies and physical evidence (fingerprints) found at the scene matched the digital timeline.
